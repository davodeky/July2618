Sunday Sermon Pointers

  • As our bodies grow, we should also be progressing.
  • Do we interrogate ourselves on whether we have grown or progressed?
  • “ When ” is now !
  • If we do not value our time, no one will be value our time.
  • We need to learn how to value time.
  • We should value temporary landmarks such as birthdays in our lives.
  • “ Preparation will open the door to the promised land. ”
  • &ldquo When preparation meets opportunity, success is inevitable.”
  • Everyday is not a day to party.
  • We should ignore mentors, coaches and spiritual oversight in our lives.
  • Every challenge that Gos brings to our lives, ride it like a horse.
  • We have been created perfect by God and He plans to raise us from one level of glow to another.
  • When we compare ourselves to others, we stop growing.
  • It is high time we turn away from that roundabout and focus on our own paths.
  • God is calling us to a new level.
  • Let us stop dreaming of the things we cannot do and start doing the things we can do and see what God will do with us next.
  • Most of the times we stand on our way of growth- we do not trust ourselves enough.
